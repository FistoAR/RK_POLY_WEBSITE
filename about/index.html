Who We Are

Leading Manufacturer of Quality Plastic Products

RK Poly Products is a premier manufacturer and supplier of high-quality plastic containers and packaging solutions based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Established in 2008, we have grown to become one of India's most trusted names in the packaging industry.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with modern machinery and technology, enabling us to produce a wide range of plastic products that meet the highest quality standards. From food-grade containers to industrial packaging solutions, we cater to diverse industries across the country.

With a dedicated team of experienced professionals and a commitment to innovation, we continuously strive to deliver products that exceed customer expectations while maintaining competitive pricing and timely delivery.
